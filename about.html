About Raj Marketing Mysore

Our Story

Raj Marketing Mysore is a family-owned business serving Mysore and surrounding areas since 2010. We specialize in providing high-quality mosquito net solutions for homes and businesses. Our journey started with a simple mission: to help families sleep peacefully and work comfortably, free from mosquito-borne worries.

Why Choose Us?

  • 15+ years of experience in the mosquito net industry
  • Trusted by hundreds of Mysore households and businesses
  • Commitment to quality products and customer satisfaction
  • Personalized solutions for every window and door
  • Excellent after-sales service and local support
  • Skilled team for expert installation & repairs

Our Promise

We believe in honest pricing, reliable service, and durable products. Your comfort and peace of mind are our top priorities. Experience hassle-free living with our premium mosquito net systems.

What makes us different?
• We use only high-grade mesh and frames, ensuring long-term protection.

• We offer both traditional and modern net systems including Velcro, Frame, Pleated, and Magnetic Mesh Doors.
• Customer satisfaction is our main focus – we’re always just a call away!
