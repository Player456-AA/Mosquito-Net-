About Us

Our Story

Raj Marketing Mysore is a family-owned business serving Mysore and surrounding areas since 2010. We specialize in providing high-quality mosquito net solutions for homes, offices, and institutions. Our journey began with a commitment to protect families from mosquitoes and other insects, making homes more comfortable and healthy.

  • Commitment to quality products and customer satisfaction
