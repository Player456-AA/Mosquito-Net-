Pleated Mosquito Net

Elegant, retractable, and space-saving protection for wide windows & balconies!

Specifications

SystemRetractable, horizontally folding pleated mesh
Mesh TypePolyester or fiberglass, pleated style
Frame MaterialAluminium, powder-coated for durability
OperationManual sliding—opens and closes smoothly
ApplicationsBalconies, french windows, wide doors

Advantages

  • Ideal for large window and door openings
  • Sleek, modern look—adds style to your interiors
  • Space-saving—retracts when not needed
  • Excellent airflow and outside view
  • Child- and pet-friendly operation
  • Prevents mosquitoes, flies, bugs

Maintenance

  • Vacuum or gently brush pleated mesh to remove dust
  • Occasionally wipe down frame and channels with a damp cloth
  • Lubricate tracks for smooth sliding if needed

Service Needed

  • Low—routine cleaning only
  • Mesh or cord can be replaced in case of damage
  • Annual inspection recommended for smooth operation

Durability

  • Mesh lasts 4–8 years (depends on usage)
  • Aluminium frame lasts 10+ years
  • Pleated mesh is weather-resistant and UV-stable
